# HerAuraé
### She shines from inside out 
HerAuraé is a user-centered women’s wellness platform designed to support health, emotional well-being, and safety through an inclusive, accessible, and empathetic digital experience.

## Problem Statement
Women's health and empowerment are crucial aspects of societal progress. Your task is to design a digital solution that empowers and supports women in various aspects of their lives, including health, education, career, well-being, and security.

## Solution Overview
HerAuraé is a user-centred digital wellness solution designed to support women through health tracking, emotional care, calm tools, and accessible support features.

##  Target Users
- Women across different life stages  
- Users seeking emotional safe wellness tools  
- Women from diverse linguistic, cultural, and accessibility backgrounds  
- First-time and non-tech users
- 
## Key Features
- **Personalized Onboarding** – Tailors the experience based on user preferences  
- **Daily Dashboard (Today View)** – Gentle check-in instead of task pressure  
- **Health & Body (Cycle Care)** – Cycle tracking with supportive suggestions  
- **Emotional Care** – Mood check-ins and journaling  
- **Calm Tools** – Guided breathing exercises for emotional regulation  
- **Support & Safety** – Talk to someone, professional help, and emergency support  

## User-Centered Design Approach
The design prioritizes empathy, simplicity, and emotional comfort. Each screen minimizes cognitive load, 
avoids judgmental language, and gives users control over their wellness journey. Visual cues, icons, 
and soft layouts ensure clarity and ease of use.

## Accessibility & Multilingual Support
HerAuraé is designed to be inclusive and accessible:
- Simple, translatable language  
- Icons paired with text for better comprehension  
- Minimal text-heavy screens  
- Multilingual-ready architecture with language selection  
- Emotionally accessible UX for diverse literacy levels

## Empowerment & Gender Equality Impact
HerAuraé empowers women by:
- Encouraging body awareness and self-understanding  
- Normalizing emotional expression  
- Providing safe access to support without stigma  
- Respecting autonomy, choice, and emotional boundaries
- 
## Future Scope
- Expanded multilingual and regional language support  
- Career and education guidance for women  
- Community-led support spaces  
- Deeper personalization and wearable integrations
